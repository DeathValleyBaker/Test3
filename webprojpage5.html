Death Valley Bakery

The Cookie Recipe.

I like to say this recipe is the magnum opus of chocolate chunk.

Recipe by Alvin Zhou

This is his YouTube channel!

Prep Time: 45 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Chill Time: 48 hours

  • 2 sticks unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 ½ cups dark brown sugar
  • 2 tsp espresso powder
  • 2 tsp kosher salt
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 ½ cups all purpose flour
  • 6 oz chocolate bar, 70% cacao minimum

Steps:

  1. Set stove on medium heat and put two sticks of unsalted butter in the skillet. Use a spatula to stir the butter. Once the butter takes on a brown hue, take it off the heat and pour the butter in a heatproof beaker. Wait for it to cool to room temperature.
  2. Get a bowl, add in the dark brown sugar, white sugar, espresso powder, salt, and baking soda. Get a whisk and mix ingredients together. It should look like brown sand.
  3. Pour the cooled butter into the bowl of sugar and whisk until just combined.
  4. Crack open and add in the two whole eggs and vanilla extract. Whisk together until the mixture becomes this ribbon-y consistency. It should also become a shade lighter than before.
  5. Add in the flour and use a spatula to gently fold the flour into the mixture until just combined.
  6. Grab your chocolate bar and use a knife to break it into smaller shards. Put the chocolate into the dough and fold it in.
  7. Place finished cookie dough into refrigerator and chill for 48 hours.
  8. Preheat oven to 300 degrees Fahrenheit. Get a light metal pan and put parchment paper on top.
  9. Get a scoop and scoop up the dough onto the pan. Place the dough into the oven for 20 minutes.
  10. After 20 minutes, safely take the cookies out of the oven and turn the oven off. Drop the cookies onto the counter and wait 2 minutes to cool.

ENJOY!
